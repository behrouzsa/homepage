About Me

I received my B.Sc. degree in Information Technology from Hamedan University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2012. Then, I started my master's studies at Iran University of Science and Technology in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics and I received my M.Sc. degree in 2015.

My research interests are Machine Learning, Classification, Feature Selection, Knowledge Optimization, and Expert Systems. Now, I would like to pursue my education career by doing a Ph.D in my interested fields.
